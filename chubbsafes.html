KING COBRA MK II

BURGLARY- & FIRE-RESISTANT PROTECTION



Features
  • A robust and durable body that boasts an overall 65 mm barrier thickness made up of a high-density monolithic casting contained within inner and outer steel casing
  • A solid composite 35mm thick door is combined with additional protection plates for crutial locking areas
  • Anchoring holes for protection against unauthorized removal
  • Solid steel moving bolts with a 28.5mm diameter affix the door to the body, while fixed “anti-heeling” bolts on both front and rear edges protect the door from prying attack

  • Tested in accordance with EN 14450 Level S2, certified by ECB•S
  • The new door built from specialized materials ensures unrivalled protection against fire in its class
  • King Cobra MKII has been tested and certified by ECB•S in accordance with EN 15659 LFS in class 60 P for up to one hour’s fire protection of paper documents.
