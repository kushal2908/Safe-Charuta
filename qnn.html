QNN Safe Manufacturing Co.Ltd is one of the leading enterprises specialized in OEM/ODM safe manufacturing in China, QNN SAFE is a. company with a rich heritage. The company was founded as a safe factory more than 80 years ago and went on to specialize in safes in 1999.QNN has expanded to a global operation with a subsidiary QNN SAFE (USA) INC in the United States in 2013.QNN product range includes hotel/residential safes, deposit safes, free-standing safes, floor safes, high-security home 8, office safes, gun safes. etc.
From 2006, Charuta Private Limited has been the Sole Distributor for QNN Safe here in Bangladesh.
